# Privacy Policy — Panchangam

_Last updated: March 2026_

Panchangam ("the app") is developed and maintained by Srikar Buddhiraju.
This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## What data we collect

### Data stored locally on your device
- Your selected city and location coordinates (for Panchangam calculations)
- Your language and theme preferences
- Personal events and To-Dos you create (tithi-based)
- Reminder settings for your events

This data **never leaves your device** unless you sign in (see below).

### Data collected when you sign in (optional)
Signing in with Google is optional. If you sign in:
- We receive your Google account name, email address, and profile photo
  from Google Sign-In.
- Your email is used to determine whether you have Pro access.
- We do **not** store your personal events on our servers — they remain
  on your device only.

### Data we do NOT collect
- We do not collect analytics or usage data.
- We do not serve ads.
- We do not share any data with third parties.
- We do not collect precise GPS location — only your manually selected city.
