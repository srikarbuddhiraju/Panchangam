## How to delete your account and data

Panchangam uses Google Sign-In (OAuth) for the Pro features. We do not maintain our own user account database — your identity is managed entirely by Google.

### Step 1 — Sign out of the app

1. Open the **Panchangam** app
2. Tap the **Settings** tab
3. Tap **Sign In** → **Sign Out**

This immediately removes your Google authentication state from the device.

### Step 2 — Delete locally stored data

All your personal events, reminders, and to-dos are stored **only on your device** (not on any server). To permanently delete them:

- **Uninstall the Panchangam app** from your device

This removes all locally stored data immediately, including personal events, reminders, to-dos, and app settings.

### Step 3 — Revoke Google Sign-In access (optional)

To remove Panchangam's OAuth access from your Google account entirely:

1. Go to [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find **Panchangam** in the list
3. Tap **Remove Access**

---

## What data is deleted

| Data | Where stored | Deleted when |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Google profile (name, email, photo) | Google's servers | You revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions |
| Auth token (sign-in state) | Your device only | You sign out or uninstall the app |
| Personal events & reminders | Your device only | You uninstall the app |
| App settings (city, language) | Your device only | You uninstall the app |

## What data is retained

We do not operate our own servers and do not retain any user data after uninstallation. Google retains OAuth access logs per their own [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
